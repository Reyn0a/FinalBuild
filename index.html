Joy

Primarily worked for: Riley Anderson
Appearance: Joy has bright yellow skin, blue eyes, and a glittery blue pixie cut. She wears a green dress and goes barefoot
Personailty: Joy is optimistic, lighthearted, and determined to find the fun in every situation. She is full of energy and excitement, and sees challenges as opportunities.

Purpose

Joy’s goal has always been to make sure Riley stays happy. She is lighthearted, optimistic and determined to find the fun in every situation. Joy sees challenges in Riley’s life as opportunities, and the less happy moments as hiccups on the way back to something great. As long as Riley is happy, so is Joy.

Accomplishments

  • Making Riley happy
  • Realizing the importance of sadness
  • Removing and reforming a better version of Riley
